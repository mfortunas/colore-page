Privacy Policy

🌎 Also available in: Português

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Colorê handles your information. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and families.

1. Who We Are

Colorê is a digital drawing and painting app designed for children, developed by Colorê App Team. For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: contact@appcolore.simplelogin.com

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Colorê is designed with children's privacy as a priority. We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Location data
  • Photos or drawings created in the app
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Any data from children under 13

3. Data Stored Locally

All drawings and content created in the app are stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to this content. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

Colorê offers an optional annual subscription. All payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or process any payment or financial information. For information on how Apple handles your payment data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Parental Controls

The app includes a parental verification mechanism before any purchase. We encourage parents and guardians to:

  • Supervise their children's use of the app
  • Enable purchase restrictions in device settings
  • Review the app's content periodically

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Colorê complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not contain advertising, does not track users, and does not share any data with third parties.

7. Third-Party Services

Colorê does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media platforms. The only third-party interaction is with Apple's App Store for subscription processing.

8. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of data breach on our end. All content remains on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security features.

9. Sharing Content

When you choose to share a drawing using the app's share feature, the content is shared directly from your device using iOS sharing capabilities. We do not intermediate or store any shared content.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make significant changes, we will notify users through the app or by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete. If you have any concerns, please contact us at contact@appcolore.simplelogin.com.
